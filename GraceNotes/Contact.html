If you would like Grace Notes to perform at your church or activity, please try to book 6 months in advance as our schedule is usually booked 6-8 months ahead.

What we ask of your organization is:
  • Enough room up front to seat 14 brass and woodwind instruments and 5 rhythm instruments that include: Drums, 3 guitars, and an electric keyboard.
  • If any furniture needs to be moved, we ask if people from your facility would move them so we don't accidentally damage any of your furniture.
  • 15 chairs (folding is fine)
  • We bring music stands and a sound system.
  • Thank you for considering The Grace Notes.
  • We come on a donation basis, we do not have a set fee.
