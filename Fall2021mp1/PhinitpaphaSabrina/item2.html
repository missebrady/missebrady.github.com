Nonbenders



Some people are nonbenders, which means they don't have any special abilities. But don't underestimate them.



Earth



Earthbending is the geokinetic ability to maniuplate earth and rock in all their various forms. Oma and Shu were the first to learn this from badgermoles.

Other styles of this are; lavabending, metalbending (invented by Toph), and sandbending.

The secret to this is to neutralize jing, which involves the person to wait and listen for the right moment to strike.

Water



Waterbending is the hydrokinetic ability to control water in all of it's various forms. Water is the element of change.

They get their power from the moon, the first waterbenders learned by observing how the moon pushed and pulled on the ocean's tides.

An alternative style is bloodbending. "Controlling the water in another body, enforcing your will over theirs. {...} Once you perfect this technique,

you can control anything, or, anyone," Hama (the first person to discover it) states.

Another form of waterbending is healing.



An image of the Northern Water Tribe.



Fire



Firebending is the pyrokinetic ability to control fire. The first human firebenders, the Sun Warriors learned this from the dragons.

Alternate styles are combustion bending, lightning generation, and redirection.

Firebending is known for its itense and aggressive tactics with adequete defensive moves.



An image of the Fire Nation Capital.



Air



Airbending is the aerokinetic ability to control and maniuplate air. Air is the element of freedom.

The Air Nomads sought peace and seperated themselves from the world, detatching themselves to solve their problems in life.

They learned this from the flying bison. Other forms can be flight and spiritual projection.

Airbeners often are purely defensive, they use their opponets energy against them because they are monks and don't believe in harming other people.
