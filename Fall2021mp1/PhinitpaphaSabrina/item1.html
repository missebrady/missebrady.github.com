Aang



Aang is an Air Nomad from the Southern Air Temple. He 12 years old and is the avatar which means his

goal in life is to master all four elements and keep peace amongst the four nations.



Sokka



Sokkka is a nonbender but a warrior from the Southern Water Tribe. His sister is Katara and he is 15 years old.



Katara



Katara is the only waterbender left from her tribe after the Fire Nation invaded and killed all benders off. She even has healing capabilities.



Toph



Toph is a 12 year old blind girl, but that didn't stop her from being one of the most greatest during her time, even able to invent metal bending.

"Even though I was born blind, I've never had any problems seeing. I see with Earthbending. It's kind of like seeing with my feet.

I feel the vibrations in the earth and I can see where everything is. You, that tree, even those ants."



Zuko



Zuko is a firebender, born as a prince into the Royal Family. His sister older sister Azula, is a firebending prodigy which is why his father disfavored him.

He was banished from the kingdom for speaking out of turn during an important meeting, and the only way he could come back is if he captured the Avatar, the fire nation's enemy.

As he chases Aang and his friends around, the tables have turned and they go from greatest enemies to friends.



Iroh



Iroh is Zuko's uncle he accompanied Zuko along his journey because he was so young when he was banished. He is a very wise person and one of his quotes are,

"In the darkest times, hope is something you give yourself."
