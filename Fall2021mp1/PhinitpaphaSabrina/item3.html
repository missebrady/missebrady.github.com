The Legend of Korra is the continued TV-series for Avatar the Last Airbender. It takes place 70 years after, in Republic City- a place with lots of diversity.

There are many nonbenders who feel they're in the minority because benders are able to compete in sports, fufill everyday jobs or even take advantage by committing crimes.

This causes a riot and revolution against benders. As this escalates, Korra a 17 year-old girl who is still learning to master all elements tries to stop this

along with her teacher Tenzin, Aang's son and her new friends Asami, Bolin, and Mako.
