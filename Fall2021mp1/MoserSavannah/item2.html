Now that you know the animals i will be talking about let me tell you a little about them.

  • Wombats
    1. They could spend up to 8 hours eating their favorite type of food,GRASS. They enjoy eating their native grasses such as tussocky'snow grass', wallaby grass, and kangaroo grass. They also eat the roots of shrubs and parts of trees.
  • Sika deer
    1. They also enjoy eating grass but they also eat many other things such as fallen leaves,brushy vegetation, herbs,fruits, fungi, ground ferns, bamboo, poison ivy, corn, and soybeans.
  • Wildebeests
    1. They as well love to eat grasses but more of a sweet, stocky type of grass.They also may tend to eat long dry grass with other herds.
  • Eastern Spotted Skunk
    1. Finally this animal enjoys to eat something other then grasses. They actually are omnivorous that eat small rodents, fruit, berries, birds,eggs,insects and larvae, lizards, snakes, and carrion
