FEAR STREET PART THREE CHARACTERS

Sarah Fier

Sarah is very hardworking. She is a good daughter and sister and cares for her family. Sarah was wrongfully blamed for the curse on Shadyside. Sarah is played by Elizabeth Scopel and Kiana Madeira.

Solomon Goode

Solomon is the ancestor of Nick Goode. He lives by himself far off from the reset of Union (town name). He is supposedly peaceful, but we learn that he, is in fact the cause of the Shadyside Curse. Solomon is played by Ashley Zukerman
