FEAR STREET PART TWO CHARACTERS

Ziggy (Christine) Berman

(Ziggy Christine) Berman, is a very sassy and arrogant. Her sister is Cindy Berman. She does not like her sister very much, but towards the end they make up and try to end the Sarah Fier Curse. Ziggy is played by Sadie Sink.

Tommy Slater

Tommy, Cindy's boyfriend works at Camp Nightwing. He becomes possessed and gets the name Camp Nightwing Killer. Tommy is played by McCabe Syle.

Alice (Unknown Last name)

Alice is a rebelious character and is the ex best friend of Cindy Berman. Alice is very sassy and knows how to fight back. Alice is played by Ryan Simpkins.

Main Killer

Camp Nightwing Killer

The Camp Nightwing Killer, AKA Tommy, is a brutal merciless killer, using an axe as a weapon of choice. The masked version of the Camp Nightwing Killer is played by Lloyd Pitts.
