FEAR STREET PART ONE CHARACTERS

Samantha Fraser

Sam is our other main protagonist. She gets targeted by the Shadyside killers. Her and Deena team up, though they aren't on the greatest terms, to defeat and stop the curse on Shadyside. Sam is played by Olivia Scott Welch.

Simon Kalivoda

Simon, one of Deena's best friends. He is one of the supporting characters. Simon is a very hardworking kid and helps provide for his family by working shifts at the local grocery store. Simon is played by Fred Hechinger.

Kate Schmidt

Kate Schmidt is another one of Deena's best friends. She is also one of the supporting characters. She is Josh's love interest. She is a over achiever, leader of all of the school clubs and she is also on the cheer squad. Kate is played by Julia Rehwald.

Josh Johnson

Josh is Deena's younger brother. He is Kate's love interest. He quickly becomes friends with Simon. He knows a lot about the Shadyside killers and helps defeat them with his knowledge. He is played by Benji Flores Jr.

Main Killer

Skull Mask

Skull Mask is the first killer we see. Under the mask is Shadyside student, Ryan Torres. Ryan is played by David W. Thompson.
